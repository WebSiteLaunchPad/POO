    Poomanity realizes that as the market cap and number holders of a token increases, so does expectations. Price and market cap are only sustainable and do not increase as long as value is continuously provided. Poomanity make no upfront promises on a roadmap unless we collectively achieve market cap milestones together (which enables us to organically use the milestone reserve to continue to create value). This model creates a shared goal by our founders and community to grow together.
